Making Cybersecurity More Accessable


Cybersecurity is an important field of computing that many people find innaccessable. However, ensuring that people understand basic cybersecurity concepts and how to protect themselves online is crutial for making the internet a safer place.

I tried to find a way to make basic cybersecurity concepts more accessable to the avarage person. I made a fun quiz that gives people various cybersecurity scenarios. At the end, the site tells them the correct answer, and more importantly, why.

You can take the quiz multiple times. Try to beat your high score!
